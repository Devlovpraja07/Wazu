Shab-e-Qadr Checklist

1. Niyat aur Dil Saaf Karna

  • Tauba ki niyat
  • Kisi ko maaf karna
  • Gila-shikwa dil se nikalna

2. Namaz

  • Tahajjud (2-12 Rakat)
  • 4 Rakat Shab-e-Qadr Namaz (3x Qadr, 7x Ikhlas)
  • Witr Namaz

3. Quran Tilawat

  • Surah Qadr
  • Surah Yaseen
  • Surah Rahman
  • Surah Mulk
  • Surah Ikhlas, Falaq, Naas

4. Zikr & Tasbihat

  • 100x SubhanAllah
  • 100x Alhamdulillah
  • 100x Allahu Akbar
  • 100x Astaghfirullah
  • 100x Darood Shareef

5. Dua

  • Apne liye & parents ke liye
  • Muslim ummah ke liye
  • "Allahumma innaka ‘afuwwun tuhibbul ‘afwa fa’fu ‘anni"

6. Sadaqah / Khairaat

  • Kuch bhi Allah ke raaste me dena

7. I’tikaf & Shukr

  • Shukr ada karna
  • Zindagi behtar banane ka irada
